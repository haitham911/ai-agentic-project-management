Prompt: One morning I wanted to have scrambled eggs
Action steps:
  1. Crack the desired number of eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add a splash of milk or cream to the eggs.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Whisk the eggs until well combined.
  5. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  6. Melt a knob of butter in the skillet.
  7. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet.
  8. Let the eggs sit for a few seconds, then gently stir with a spatula.
  9. Continue to stir and fold the eggs until they reach your desired consistency.
  10. Remove the skillet from heat and transfer the scrambled eggs to a plate.
  11. Serve and enjoy your scrambled eggs.

Prompt: I need to bake a chocolate cake
Action steps:
  1. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified in the recipe.
  2. Grease and flour the cake pans.
  3. Mix together the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt) in a bowl.
  4. In a separate bowl, cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  6. Mix in vanilla extract.
  7. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with milk.
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pans.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for the time specified in the recipe.
  10. Check for doneness by inserting a toothpick into the center of the cake - if it comes out clean, the cake is done.
  11. Remove the cakes from the oven and let them cool in the pans for a few minutes.
  12. Invert the cakes onto a wire rack to cool completely before frosting.

Prompt: I want to make a cup of coffee
Action steps:
  1. Boil water.
  2. Grind coffee beans to a medium-coarse consistency.
  3. Place a coffee filter in a drip cone or coffee maker.
  4. Add the ground coffee to the filter.
  5. Pour hot water over the coffee grounds in a circular motion, allowing it to bloom.
  6. Let the coffee drip through the filter into a cup.
  7. Add sugar, milk, or cream to taste.
  8. Stir and enjoy your cup of coffee.
